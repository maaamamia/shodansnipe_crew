# Code of Conduct

## Ahoy, Matey! 🏴‍☠️

Welcome to the Shodan Snipe Crew—where recon be an art form and the digital seas be vast and full of secrets to uncover.

## The Pirate's Code

Aye, we sail by one simple rule: **Use ethically.**

Whether ye be scannin' the network waters, chartin' attack surfaces, or huntin' for vulnerabilities in the digital deep, remember this—there be a difference between a cunning captain and a scallywag.

## The Crew's Creed

We be a fellowship of security researchers and digital explorers. We value:

- **Respect** - Treat fellow crew members with honor and respect
- **Collaboration** - Share yer knowledge and help yer mates navigate treacherous waters
- **Integrity** - Sail true and keep yer word
- **Inclusivity** - All seekers of knowledge be welcome aboard
- **Camaraderie** - We be stronger together

## Fair Winds & Following Seas

Use this tool with honor. Chart yer course wisely. And remember—the greatest treasure be the knowledge ye gain along the way.
